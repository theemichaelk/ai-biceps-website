Frequently asked questions

How is GMB optimization different from claiming my profile?
Claiming a profile is step zero. Optimization is the ongoing engineering of categories, content, reviews, photos, and citations that actually moves rankings. Most Texas B2B profiles are claimed but under-optimized and suppressed.
Can you fix a suspended Google Business Profile?
Yes. We identify policy violations, duplicate conflicts, and NAP issues, then execute a safe reinstatement path. Fixing root causes prevents repeat suspensions.
How many reviews do we need to rank in Texas metros?
There is no universal number. We benchmark against Map Pack competitors in your specific market and build velocity targets. Most clients aim for 2 to 4 new reviews per week with detailed project context.
Do you manage Google Posts for us?
Yes. Weekly posts are core to the Velocity System. We handle copy, offers, project spotlights, and keyword alignment unless you prefer a collaborative approval workflow.
